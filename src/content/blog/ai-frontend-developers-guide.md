## What is AI in Frontend Development?

AI in frontend development means leveraging machine learning models — particularly large language models (LLMs) — to automate, accelerate, and enhance the way you build user interfaces. In 2026, this spans:

- **Code Generation**: AI writes components, hooks, and pages from natural language descriptions
- **Code Review**: Automated PR reviews catch bugs, style issues, and security flaws
- **Design Systems**: AI generates design tokens, component variants, and documentation
- **Testing**: AI creates test cases and audits accessibility automatically
- **Personalization**: AI tailors UI and content to individual users in real time

For a broader look at how AI is reshaping the industry, see [Frontend AI Trends in 2026](/blog/frontend-ai-trends-2026/) and [The Evolution of Frontend Development in 2026](/blog/frontend-evolution-2026/).
